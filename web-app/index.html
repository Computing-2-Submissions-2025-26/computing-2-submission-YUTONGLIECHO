How to play

Goal: get five of your stones in a row — horizontal, vertical, or diagonal. Six or more (an overline) also wins.

Turns: players alternate. On your turn, click an empty intersection to place one stone, then optionally fire one skill, then end your turn.

Placing: click a crossing point, or move focus with the arrow keys and press Enter or Space.

Skills: after placing, use one ready skill. Targeted skills (Yeet, Finders) need you to then click an enemy stone. Most skills end your turn and go on cooldown.

Modes: in Player vs AI you are Black against the computer. In Local 2-player two humans share the device, Black then White.

  • Yeet Meteor — remove an enemy stone. (cd 5)
  • Finders Keepers — move an enemy stone to a random empty cell. (cd 4)
  • Spring Cleaning — remove 1–3 random enemy stones. (cd 7)
  • Absolute Zero — freeze the foe's skills next turn. (cd 5)
  • Corporate — swap every stone's colour. (once)
  • Table Flip — clear the whole board. (once)
